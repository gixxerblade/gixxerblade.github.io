About Me
I am retired from the United States Marine Corps and live near Camp Lejeune, NC. I am a native of California but decided to make NC my permanent home after retiring. I am currently using my G.I. Bill to complete a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology with a focus on Cybersecurity at Excelsior College. Since I have retired I have taken on some new challenges.
